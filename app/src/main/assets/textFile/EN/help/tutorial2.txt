To install the program, first of all, you need to assemble the PC and go to the menu for interacting with the PC, to go, click on the person sitting at the computer and you will go to the menu for interacting with the PC, then click on the button "Interacting with the PC", after all the conditions must be fulfilled

1.Buy the program in the store
2.The drive must have enough space
3.The program must be compatible with the pc
Note: If you replace the drive while modifying the PC, then all programs will be deleted
